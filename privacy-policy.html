This Privacy Policy explains how FitPilot collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data.

1. Data We Collect

Account identifiers, profile details, fitness goals, nutrition logs, workout logs, sleep and recovery metrics, and data shared by connected third-party platforms after user authorization.

2. Purpose of Processing

We process data to generate personalized workout and nutrition recommendations, display progress history, and improve service quality and safety.

3. Legal Basis and Consent

For third-party health platform data, we only access data after explicit OAuth authorization by the user. Users may revoke access at any time.

4. Data Sharing

FitPilot does not sell personal data. We do not share identifiable health data with advertising networks.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained during active account usage and removed within 30 days after a verified deletion request unless law requires otherwise.

6. Security

We apply access control, encryption in transit, and operational safeguards to reduce unauthorized access risk.

7. User Rights

Users can request access, correction, export, and deletion of personal data by contacting support.

8. Contact

Email: support@fitpilot.app
