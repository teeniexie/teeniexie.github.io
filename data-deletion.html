Data Deletion Instructions

返回首页

Last updated: 2026-03-03

Users can request data deletion at any time.

1. What to Send

Email support@fitpilot.app with subject Data Deletion Request and include your account email and request date.

2. Verification

We will verify request ownership to prevent unauthorized deletion.

3. Processing Timeline

We process validated deletion requests within 7 business days and notify completion by email.

4. What Gets Deleted

Profile data, nutrition logs, workout records, and synced third-party health data under your account.

5. Third-Party Access Revocation

You can revoke third-party OAuth connections directly in account settings of connected platforms.
